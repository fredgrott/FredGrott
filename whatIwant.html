What I Want

While, those firms that contract out for specific developer services somewhat know what android application they are building and the android application skills required it seems that startups as far as top managment tend to not take the time to find out and are asking blind.

Thus, this a polite helpful way of stating get an effing clue. Specifically you want a mobile applications engineer that has a good background in java and agile. How do you tell?

Specifically, right now with Google spear-heading a change from the Ant build system to Gradle certain tools have failed to catch up such as Continuous Integration Servers, CodeQA coverage Servers, and Test Runner Servers. The specific obstacle is that Gradle introduced concepts of native binary, product and flavor but their not fully implemented yet and thus Google came up with their own implementation of that and android sourcesets as a placeholder.

Thus, I the mobile applications engineer, have determined if I start doing a new implementation of codeqa tasks and module/project dashboards within the project build scripts that I can eventually once that is complete eventually use similar techniques and tool fill out rest of the tools with my android specific implementatyions so that I do not have to wait as the underlying obstacle described above with it not fully implemented the gradle way in the gradle sdk will be that way for about 18 to 24 months.

And yes, I am fully aware that given the market size of the android development tools and services market which is about 500 billion that I could in fact build a start focusing on that window opportunity. Thus, in engaging in communication with me you are not dealign with a beginning or intemediate developer.

Your CTO/Engineer would love to find some mobile applications engineer that is taking it upon themselves to determine which problems need to be solved now and in the future and coming up with a mission and objectives of that mission and executing on them. So how do you get me?

First, you have to determine if you want me. The talk to figure out if you want me is not working as there is 50 plus entities asking per week. Ever hear that Blue Brother line of 'I am on a mission from God'? Well, after I complete the beginning of the codeqa tool I am than proceeding to implement an AUT/FSM tool(its automating testing of the UI components) and along the way I am determining the how to write the CS MS/PhD level journal papers and will be completing the process of getting them published. So you can see that if all the 50 entities per week are attmepting to talk to me with none of them determining if they want me that will just not work as far as time constraints, etc.

Now, we come down to what I might want. Because of a startup directly lying about having competed the seed capital collection phase, I lost potential project contract payments that was going to be used to buy specific equipment and relocate me to Lake county Indiana so that I would only be 25 minutes from downtown via South Shore.

I have to work 26 weeks at a certain 1099 weekly fee, paid weekly, to finance the equipment purchases I need to make and to finance the relocation to Lake county Indiana. Thus, the requirement to work remotely and under weekly 1099 is somewhat temporary and it will result in a relocation that will allow me to work-on-site in Chicago as most of the Chicago loccations will be reachable through the SouthShore and Metra combinations with travel times ranging from 25 to 56 minutes.

And yes, there are those of you reading this that upon hearing that may choose to say we are not a fit beccause of the temporary 1099 remote requirement. That is okay, because if we are not a fit than we are not a fit and beside you will be kicking yoruself in 12 months wishing you had said yes anyway. The entity that says yes, will of course will be dancing-in-the-streets.

For those of you that may have decided that you might want to consider granting the 26-weeks developing-remotely at a 1099 rate temporary terms to get a more pernament full-time solution it is far easier for me to insist that you make a commitment to travel to my location to proceed with a discussion as I want to show you some immediate stuff that I am working on as part of the discussion as its not building tools just to build tools as they are getting used to build applications. For most of you will not be much of travel commitment because the place we will meet at is only 45 minutes from downtown Chicago.

Concluding, yes it is a bit long. But, we need things focused on the missions and tasks of both you and myself so both get what we want.
